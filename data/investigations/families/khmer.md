## Investigation

The METADATA.pb for Khmer has no `source` block. The font was present in the initial commit of google/fonts (`90abd17b4`), with an additional `chmod -x` commit (`49fbebd3d`).

The Khmer font is available as part of the KhmerOS project. The cached repository at `/mnt/shared/upstream_repos/fontc_crater_cache/librefonts/khmer` contains TTX dumps of the binary font.
